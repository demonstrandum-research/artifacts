2. CONVENTION: "deviation"
   - WoW treats "variance" (conj. 38: "The variance of the distance matrix...",
     conj. 143: "variance of positive eigenvalues <= size / average distance")
     and "deviation" (conj. 39, 40, 140, 154, 244, 253) as distinct invariants;
     "standard deviation" is spelled out for degree sequences (conj. 27, 812,
     813). Reading: deviation = sqrt(variance) = population standard deviation.
   - The only public machine formalization of conjecture 154 is
     Roucairol-Cazenave: repo RoucairolMilo/refutationGBR, file
     models/conjectures/GenerateGraph.rs, block `if CONJECTURE == 154` --
     comment "//dev eigen <= nb sommet/distance moyenne"; computes
     invariants::std_dev over ALL adjacency eigenvalues and divides n by the
     mean of ALL n^2 entries of the distance matrix (diagonal zeros included).
     tools/calc.rs `std` = population standard deviation (divide by len, sqrt).
     (local copies: GenerateGraph.rs, calc.rs)
   - HISTORICAL CONVENTION (Favaron-Maheo-Sacle, "Some eigenvalue properties
     in graphs (conjectures of Graffiti - II)", Discrete Math. 111 (1993)
     197-220; WoW credits conj. 140 "deviation of eigenvalues <= harmonic" to
     FMS): ScienceDirect copy is paywalled (403), but the Academia.edu mirror
     https://www.academia.edu/25437446/Some_eigenvalue_properties_in_graphs_conjectures_of_Graffiti_II_
     (accessed 2026-06-11, both by Codex and independently by this agent)
     yields, OCR warts and all:
       * p.198 Definitions: "The mean distance mu of G is the average value
         1/n(n-1) sum_{x,y in V} d(x,y) taken over all ordered pairs of
         vertices."  => distinct-pairs convention l = 2W/(n(n-1)).
       * p.211 (Thm 2.36 context): "deviation of eigenvalues of the Laplacian
         = sigma(n) = ((sum d_i^2 + d-bar^2)/2)^1/2" [OCR-garbled] -- visibly
         a quadratic/square-root (root-mean-square) deviation formula, i.e.
         standard deviation, NOT mean absolute deviation.
     FMS does not mention conjecture 154 itself.
   - HONEST RESIDUAL CAVEAT: no surviving source spells out Graffiti's
     "deviation" definition next to conjecture 154 itself. If "deviation"
     were instead MEAN ABSOLUTE deviation (= energy/n, since the eigenvalue
     mean is 0), the lollipop family does NOT violate the conjecture: MAD
     non-violation at all three certificate instances is PROVEN in exact
     arithmetic in this bundle (Sturm-certified energy enclosures + exact
     Koolen-Moulton route + mpmath dps=60 sign separation ~ +3 to +4.1).
     The kill is therefore claimed ONLY under the standard-deviation reading
     -- which is the reading of every known formalization (FMS 1993, R-C
     2024/2025 code).

3. CONVENTION: "average distance"
   - WoW comment on conjecture 536 (decoded): "The average distance of this
     graph is 3/2" (Paley graphs) -- true only under the distinct-pairs
     convention l = 2W/(n(n-1)). This is the document's own usage.
   - Roucairol-Cazenave code uses l = 2W/n^2 (mean over the full matrix).
   - Certificate instances lollipop(50,70) (n=120) and lollipop(72,72) (n=144)
     violate BOTH conventions; lollipop(48,70) (n=118) violates only the
     distinct-pairs convention. The conclusion is convention-independent.
